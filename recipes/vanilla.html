Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

I found this recipe in a place none other than the internet.

Pretty quick and easy to make up if you have the machinery to do so.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Whole Milk
  • 2 Cups of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 2 Tsp of Vanilla

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add in ice cream maker/mixer.
  3. Mix for 20-25 minutes or until desired consistency.
