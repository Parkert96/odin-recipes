Banana Bread

Description

This recipe was given to me by my mother-in-law. She has always been the best at making it.

Banana bread is a great treat or dessert. Make it up and store it away for an indulgence any time of the week!

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup of Butter
  • 2/3 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla
  • 2 Cups of Flour

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix ingredients to desired consistency
  3. Grease the bread pan
  4. Bake for 45 minutes uncovered
  5. Then bake for 15 min covered by tin foil
