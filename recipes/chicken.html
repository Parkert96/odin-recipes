Balsamic Chicken

Description

This is a family recipe perfected by my grandma and passed down to her kids.

Especially good for the holidays but it can be eaten year round.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup of Olive Oil
  • 1/4 Cup of Balsamic Vinegar
  • Fresh Rosemary
  • 1 Tsp of Salt
  • 1 Tsp of Pepper
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Green Onion
  • Goat Cheese
  • 8 Chicken Breasts

Steps

  1. Blend the oils, rosemary, salt, pepper, and garlic in a blender for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Rub on chicken and marinate over night or at least 8 hours. Only use half of the marinade.
  3. BBQ the chicken
  4. Saute the mushrooms, chop the onions, and crumble the cheese
  5. Put the mushrooms, onions, and crumbles on the chicken.
  6. Heat the rest of the marinade and pour it on the chicken.
